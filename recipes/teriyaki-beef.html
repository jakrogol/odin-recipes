Teriyaki Beef

Description

A super simple savory dish, this recipe takes only one skillet and ten minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 cup finely diced onion
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger (1/4 teaspoon dried ground ginger)
  • 3/4 cup thick teriyaki sauce

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat for about 1 minute.
  2. Add beef, onion, garlic, and ginger to the skillet. Cook, breaking up the meat as you stir, until the beef is no longer pink and the onion is tender (about 5-7 minutes). Drain off excess grease.
  3. Stir teriyaki sauce into the beef mixture. Bring to a low simmer and cook for an additional 3-5 minutes, just until the sauce thickens slightly.
  4. Serve over rice, in tortillas, or in lettuce wraps. Garnish with optional toppings.
