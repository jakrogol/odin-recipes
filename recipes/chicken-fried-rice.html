Chicken Fried Rice

Description

This chicken fried rice recipe is very easy and tasty! It's basically cooked chicken, rice, soy sauce, and shredded egg stir-fried together. For best results, use leftover cooked rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat egg and water together in small bowl. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat
  3. add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set aside.
  5. Heat oil in the same skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.
  6. Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes
  7. Stir in shredded egg. Serve hot.
